Suy Nhược

Heal

Cooldown: 120s

Nearby allies instantly restore 15% Max HP and increase 15% Movement Speed for 2s

Sprint

Cooldown: 100s

Removes all Slow effects and gain a diminishing (70% - 30%) Movement Speed for 10s

Punish

Cooldown: 30s

Deals (1350 - 2750) True Damage to a nearby Wildling / Minion and stun them for 1s
Frostbite: Simultaneously cast on a nearby enemy, dealing (200 - 410) True Damage with 50% Slow for 2s

Execute

Cooldown: 90s

Instantly attacks nearby enemies, deals True Damage = Target's 16% Lost HP

Flicker

Cooldown: 120s

Teleports a certain distance in the target direction

Roar

Cooldown: 60s

Increases 25% Resistance and 10% Damage Dealt, while healing 2% Max HP every second over 6s

Daze

Cooldown: 90s

Stuns all nearby enemies for 0.75s, then reduces Targets' 20% Movement Speed, (28 + Lv*2) Armor, and (14 + Lv) Magic Armor for 2.5s

Purify

Cooldown: 120s

Removes all Debuffs and Control Effects. Gains Super Armor for 1.5s

Enfeeble

Cooldown: 90s

Reduces nearby enemies' 20% Damage Dealt and gains 20% Damage Reduction for 4s
